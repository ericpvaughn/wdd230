Audience Description

The target audience is the common citizen of the Manchester community, who are interested in the working events of the area. Typically above 25 years of age, resident, stable job, and willing to help others. They are invested in creating a working community through whatever resources available.

Personas

Persona 1: Richard, recent retiree

Richard has recently entered retirement and has permanently moved to his residence in Manchester, Vermont. He's still wanting to be active and help people with their businesses, as he spent his whole life in the working class. He checks out the Chamber of Commerce website to see what events and services are happening so he can participate.

Persona 2: Bridger, recent college grad

Bridger has recently returned back to her hometown, Manchester, Vermont. She wants to start making local contacts and begin a business network in the area. She visits the Chamber of Commerce website to see who she can contact to get a list of business owners and their businesses so she can begin her work.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Richard has recently learned of a person in town named Bridger who is looking to bring business owners together. He is wondering if the website will offer any details on events she may be hosting to bring them together.

Scenario 2: Bridger wants to know how long certain businesses have been open without needing to individually contact each business owner. She wants to know if the website offers any of that information

Scenario 3: Richard wonders if it's possible to simply become part of the Chamber of Commerce to streamline his business knowledge. He wonders if there are application forms online.

Scenario 4: Bridger is having trouble setting up an event she wants the city to sponsor for a festival to showcase the local businesses. She wants to see if the website offers a contact list or calendar for planning such an event.

Scenario 5: Richard is now participating in an event where local businesses come together to showcase their products and services. He wants to find out what he needs to do to open his own business that offers management training.
