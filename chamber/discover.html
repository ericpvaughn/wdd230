Original Site of the ITVFest

Manchester, Vermont is often used as the staple for Autumn postcards and maple syrup distribution in the US.

Who knew such a small town has such an impact?

As a historic town, Manchester holds buildings that date back to the Revolutionary War!

Here is the Bennington Courthouse, built in 1822.

Marsh Tavern is the original meetingplace for the Green Mountain Boys, who were key to taking back Fort Ticonderoga.

Now you can relax and drink some fine food and spirits where they sat.

Manchester is known best for the various, high-quality ski resorts nearby. Take advantage of this postercard town during the Winter when the snow is everywhere!

One of Manchester's main attractions is the Northshire Bookstore, a place for food, books, and little souvenirs. Many come here just to lounge for the afternoon.
